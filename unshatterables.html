The Unshatterables

A closeted gay man befriends an escort. A widower still saves a seat for his wife at sunset. A woman grapples with the aftermath of childbirth. Teenage enemies come together in a ghostly way. Two young women fall in love in a bustling city.

Told through a series of short stories, The Unshatterables unpacks trauma, hope, and healing in immersive snippets of humanity and the love that binds us. Unfolding in multiple timelines, in this diverse series of love stories—romantic, platonic, and familial—devotion, affection, growth, and recovery triumph over the many pitfalls of life. True love, in any form, endures. It is unshatterable.

Audience: Short story/fiction readers, relationships, LGBTQIA+, healing
