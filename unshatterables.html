The Unshatterables

The Unshatterables is a collection of short stories following families, romantic relationships, and those grieving after death. With a diverse series of love stories—young love, widowed love, same-sex couples, parent-child relationships, and siblings.

The Unshatterables unpacks trauma, hope, and healing. Miranda Faye Dillon spotlights the many pitfalls of life—loss, heartbreak, and pain—and highlights the many grand moments—love, growth, and recovery.

Audience: Short story/fiction readers, relationships, LGBTQIA+, healing
